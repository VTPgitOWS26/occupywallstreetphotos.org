Occupy Wall Street Photos

A Documentary Archive of the Occupy Movement
Across the United States

Created by documentary photographer Vance Petrunoff

The Project

Between 2011 and 2012, I traveled across the United States documenting Occupy encampments as they emerged in cities large and small.

What began as a photographic journey evolved into a documentary archive preserving one of the defining grassroots movements of the early twenty-first century.

This website serves as the guide to that archive. Each Occupy camp links directly to its complete photographic collection on Flickr, organized by the exact date of my visit.
